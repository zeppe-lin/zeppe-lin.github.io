Welcome to Zeppe-Lin

Introduction

Minimal. Source-based. Built to be understood.

Zeppe-Lin is a GNU/Linux distribution for x86-64 systems, forked from CRUX and forged in the spirit of KISS — Keep It Simple, Stupid.

It emphasizes clarity, minimalism, and direct control of the computing environment.

Current Release

Zeppe-Lin v1.2
Released January 9, 2026

This release focused on consolidation of core primitives, log management standardization, and ABI alignment.

Highlights:

  • OpenSSL 3.5.4 ABI bump
  • Logging standardized with logrotate
  • Cron execution unified under run-parts;
    run-one introduced for simpler job control
  • Website redesign at <zeppe-lin.org>

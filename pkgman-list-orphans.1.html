NAME

pkgman-list-orphans - list orphaned packages on your system

SYNOPSIS

pkgman list-orphans [-v]

DESCRIPTION

Print list of orphaned packages on your system. "Orphaned" are packages what are not present in the packages sources directories configured in /etc/pkgman.conf as pkgsrcdir directories.

OPTIONS

-v

Also show the ones that depends on orphaned (or even completely missing) packages.

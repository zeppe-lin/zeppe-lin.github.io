# Welcome to Zeppe-Lin {.unnumbered}

![Artwork for v1.2 --- Ghosts in the Rig](https://github.com/zeppe-lin/artwork/blob/master/v1.2.png?raw=true)

## Introduction {.unnumbered}

**Minimal. Source-based. Built to be understood.**

Zeppe-Lin is a GNU/Linux distribution for x86-64 systems,
forked from [CRUX](https://crux.nu/) and forged in the spirit
of KISS --- *Keep It Simple, Stupid*.

It emphasizes clarity, minimalism, and direct control of the
computing environment.

## Current Release {.unnumbered}

**Zeppe-Lin v1.2**  ("Ghosts in the Rig")  
Released January 9, 2026

This is a structural maintenance release focused on logging, cron
execution, and ABI alignment.

Highlights:

- OpenSSL 3.5.4 ABI bump
- Logging standardized with `logrotate`
- Cron execution unified under `run-parts`; `run-one` introduced for
  simpler job control
- Tooling manual pages migrated to scdoc and improved
- Website redesign at <zeppe-lin.org>
- Kernel updated from 6.16 series to 6.18
